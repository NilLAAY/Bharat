Down South

Coorg,KN

Coorg, Karnataka's "Scotland of India," is a hill station famed for its coffee estates, misty mountains, and cascading waterfalls. This nature lover's paradise offers adventure (trekking, rafting) and relaxation amidst stunning scenery.

Ooty and Coonoor,TN

The Nilgiri Hills, encompassing Ooty and Coonoor, provide a tranquil escape for city dwellers. Lush greenery, misty mountains, and serene lakes or verdant tea estates offer a haven of peace, ideal for rejuvenation and reconnection with nature.

Hampi,KN

Steeped in history and imbued with a mystical atmosphere, Hampi in Karnataka offers a unique escape for the soul. Journey back in time amidst the awe-inspiring ruins of the Vijayanagara empire, or find serenity amidst ancient temples and sacred sites. Hampi's captivating landscape, dotted with granite boulders and bathed in the golden hues of the setting sun, provides a transformative experience.

Mahabalipuram,TN

Tranquil beaches and captivating history collide in Mahabalipuram, Tamil Nadu. Unwind on pristine shores, or delve into the past with ancient temples and intricate sculptures. This unique blend of serenity and heritage offers a transformative escape for weary travelers.

Pondicherry,PY

Steeped in colonial charm and imbued with a laid-back vibe, Pondicherry offers a unique escape for the soul. Wander through French-inspired streets lined with colorful cafes, or find serenity on pristine beaches. Pondicherry's captivating blend of French heritage and Indian culture provides a tranquil escape, perfect for rejuvenation and exploration.

Kochi,KL

Kerala's Kochi, a vibrant tapestry woven with history and spice, offers a captivating escape. Explore Fort Kochi's colonial past or wander historic streets lined with spice shops. Cruise the scenic backwaters or unwind on pristine beaches. Kochi's unique blend of cultural heritage and natural beauty provides a captivating experience for the soul.
